Introduction

Captain Vikram Batra was a soldier of India honored with Param Vir Chakra. He got this honor posthumously in 1999. Captain Vikram Batra was born on September 9, 1974 in Baggarga in Palampur district of Himachal Pradesh. His father's name is GM Batra and mother's name is Kamalakanta Batra. Vikram joined the Indian Military Academy in 1996. Vikram was involved in 13 Jammu and Kashmir Rifles on December 6, 1997, on Lieutenant's post. On June 1, 1999, his detachment was sent to Kargil war. Vikram was made Captain at the same time after winning the Hump and Rocky Nab locations. After this, the responsibility of releasing the most important 5140 peak right above the Srinagar-Leh road was also given to Captain Vikram Batra. In spite of being an extremely remote area, Vikram Batra took the peak with his colleagues at 30.30 am on June 20, 1999.

Captain Vikram Batra Famous as Shershah

When Captain Vikram Batra said that his victory through radio from this peak was 'This heart mange More', he did not even mention the army but his name in the whole of India. At the same time Vikram's name was given as the name of Sher Shah as well as the name of 'Kargil Sher'. The next day, in the top 5140, with the Indian flag, the photograph of Vikram Batra and his team came in the media, then everyone became crazy. After this the army started the campaign to capture the peak 4875. It was also handed over to Vikram.

Winning slogan by Captain Vikram Batra

Kargil war was created in the warrior slogan 'Ye Dil Maange More', these lines were seen in Kargil as enemies for the enemies and on each side it was heard only 'Yeh Dil mange More'.

Captain Vikram Batra of Param Vir Chakra

Captain Batra, who completed his studies from Chandigarh, joined the Indian Military Academy. As a lieutenant, he became the commissioned officer of the Indian Army and then led 13 Jammu and Kashmir Rifles in the Kargil war. For his never-forgetting contribution to the Kargil War, he was honored with the highest honor of Param Vir Chakra in August 1999.
